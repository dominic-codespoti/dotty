# Sample ANSI Test Sequences

## Basic Colors (SGR)
- Red foreground: ESC[31m
- Green foreground: ESC[32m
- Blue foreground: ESC[34m
- Reset: ESC[0m

## Text Attributes
- Bold: ESC[1m
- Dim: ESC[2m
- Italic: ESC[3m
- Underline: ESC[4m
- Blink: ESC[5m
- Reverse: ESC[7m
- Hidden: ESC[8m
- Strikethrough: ESC[9m

## 256 Colors
- Format: ESC[38;5;Nm (foreground)
- Format: ESC[48;5;Nm (background)
- Where N is 0-255

## TrueColor
- Format: ESC[38;2;R;G;Bm (foreground)
- Format: ESC[48;2;R;G;Bm (background)

## Cursor Movement
- Up: ESC[nA
- Down: ESC[nB
- Forward: ESC[nC
- Back: ESC[nD
- Next line: ESC[nE
- Prev line: ESC[nF
- Horizontal abs: ESC[nG
- Position: ESC[row;colH

## Erase
- Display: ESC[J or ESC[0J (cursor to end)
- Display: ESC[1J (start to cursor)
- Display: ESC[2J (entire display)
- Line: ESC[K or ESC[0K (cursor to end)
- Line: ESC[1K (start to cursor)
- Line: ESC[2K (entire line)

## Scrollback
- Scroll up: ESC[nS
- Scroll down: ESC[nT
